Chicken & Bacon Caesar Salad

Succulent chicken & crispy bacon on a layer of romaine gem lettuce, with cucumber, spring onions, cherry tomatoes drizzled in a caesar dressing and croutons

Ingredients:

  • 500g cooked chicken
  • 250g cooked bacon
  • Caesar dressing
  • Parmaggiano Reggiano
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Spring onions
  • Cucumber (diced)
  • Romaine lettuce
  • Croutons
  • Cheese (optional)

Method:

  1. Prepare and wash all salad
  2. Toss salad in a mixing bowl, adding the bacon and chicken
  3. Once tossed together, throw in some parmaggiano regianno
  4. Drizzle over Caesar dressing
  5. Add croutons
  6. Serve and Enjoy!
