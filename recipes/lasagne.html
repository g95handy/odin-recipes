Lasagne

Beef bolognese with a tomato and cream sauce, layered between pasta sheets

Ingredients:

  • 500g beef mince
  • 500g tinned tomatoes
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Jar of cream sauce
  • Lasagne Sheets
  • 1 onion - white
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 celery sticks
  • Herbs for flavour (oregano, basil)
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Garlic bread and salad (optional)

Method:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 200C
  2. Add oil to a pan, on a medium heat
  3. Finely chop celery, carrots and onions and add to pan
  4. Chop and add garlic
  5. Once vegetables softened, add all the mince to the pan and cook until brown
  6. Add the tinned tomatoes and simmer until mince is cooked
  7. Get an overproof dish (large), add a layer of the bolognese to the dish, then a layer of cream, cover with a layer of lasagne.
  8. Repeat the process until the desired amount of lasagne is reached
  9. Cook in oven for 35-45 minutes, adding garlic bread halfway through
  10. Serve & Enjoy!
